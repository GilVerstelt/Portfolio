Gip - webschop

As part of my secondary school final project, I was tasked with establishing a travel agency from scratch. I named this agency Traverstelt, and a crucial component of the project was developing an online shop for travel guides using Bootstrap. This undertaking required a combination of design skills, technical proficiency, and attention to detail.

Leveraging the power of Bootstrap, a versatile front-end framework, I embarked on designing the layout and visual elements of the online shop. I utilized Bootstrap's responsive grid system, ensuring that the shop would adapt seamlessly to various screen sizes and devices. By utilizing its pre-built components, such as navigation menus, product cards, and carousels, I was able to establish a consistent and visually appealing interface.
