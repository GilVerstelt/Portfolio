This endeavor required a combination of creativity, technical skills, and attention to detail.

Once the design phase was complete, I transitioned to the development stage. I utilized my coding skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to transform the design into a fully functional website. HTML provided the foundation for structuring the website's content, while CSS was utilized to apply styles, including fonts, colors, and layouts, to enhance the visual appeal.
